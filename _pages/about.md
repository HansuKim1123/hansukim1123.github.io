Hansu Kim is an Assistant Professor of [School of Mechanical Engineering](https://www.changwon.ac.kr/sme/) at [Changwon National University](https://www.changwon.ac.kr/), Changwon, Korea. He received the bachelorâ€™s degree and the Ph.D. degree in [Automotive Engineering](https://ae.hanyang.ac.kr/) from [Hanyang University](https://www.hanyang.ac.kr/), Seoul, Korea in Feb 2015 and Aug 2021, respectively.
